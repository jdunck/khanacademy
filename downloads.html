Here are some options for downloading Khan Academy videos and exercises for use offline, or in instances where youtube is blocked.

Note: we have little or no experience with most of these, so they aren't officially supported.

Videos

  • Khan Academy on iTunes U.
  • Khan Academy on archive.org Compressed playlist packages in flv format, current as of July 2010.
  • Khan Academy on a Stick Math and Science lectures from khanacademy.org, as available on September 2010.
  • Khan Academy on curriki Download individual videos where youtube is blocked.
  • Khan Academy: A Classroom In Your Pocket by Irynsoft. An app for the ipod/iphone/ipad.
  • Downloadable Material issue on code.google.com. Check here for updates.

Videos and Exercises

  • Offline videos and exercises for windows. A packaged up development server. Unzip it and click on run.bat, which will start up the server and load the local version of the Khan Academy homepage in your browser. Next go to the download_scripts directory, and click on the playlist that you want to download. Once the script is complete, the videos in that playlist will work when you click on them in the browser.
  • Offline use issue on code.google.com. Check here for updates.
